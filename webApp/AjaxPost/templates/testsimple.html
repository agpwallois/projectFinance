Overview Key features Benefits Industry use cases

Shango is an AI-infused integrated financial modeling and project structuring solution that turns raw data into actionable insights

Shango provides comprehensive financial modeling and project structuring solutions for renewable energy. Trusted by investors, banks, and developers, our software offers a dependable approach to project evaluation, fostering efficient collaboration. Monitor the real-time financial performance of your renewable energy assets, enabling informed decision-making. Impress stakeholders with professional reports and streamline portfolio management within a unified platform. Predict outcomes with flexible and AI-infused forecasting. Analyze large-scale and fine-grain what-if scenarios in real-time so you can pivot quickly. With Shango’s data integration capabilities, access data from multiple sources via an intuitive web interface or familiar Excel spreadsheets. Its in-memory database (TM1) analyzes big data fast to give real-time insights.

Key features

Simplicity and Error Prevention

Shango's user-friendly interface is crafted for ease of use, ensuring that even those new to financial modeling can navigate its features without fear of errors. Our platform automatically updates financial parameters, ensuring your models are always accurate and up to date.

Automated Analysis and Optimization

Our platform doesn't just build models; it enhances them. Shango automatically analyzes projects, optimizing financial structures to avoid cash traps and implement effective hedging strategies, ensuring your project's financial health.

Flexibility Across Borders

With Shango, the daunting task of adapting financial models to each country's fiscal and accounting context becomes a thing of the past. Simply select your project's country, and the specific calculation modules appear, tailored to local requirements.

Decision-Making Assistance

Negotiating project contracts can be a murky business. Shango acts as your intelligent financial assistant, helping you analyze and negotiate project and financing contracts with a clear understanding of the expected returns.

Intelligent Financial Structuring

Project financing demands a sophisticated financial structure, which is where Shango shines. Our platform suggests the most relevant and optimized financial structures automatically, aligning with both banks' and sponsors' needs.

End-to-End Solution

From solar and wind farm development to operational project monitoring, Shango aims to provide a comprehensive solution. We cover all stages, offering both financial negotiation models and operational tracking models to ensure debt coverage ratios are met.
